Our Services Cover Every Step Of The Bathroom/Kitchen Renovation Process, From Safely Removing Previous Tiling Jobs To Leveling, Waterproofing And Tiling The Surfaces, Every Step Is Covered.




Tiling

Our bread and butter. All tiling work is completed professionally with no short-cuts taken, we are able to supply ranges of tiles, glues, grouts, sealers and tile trim.


Full Room Renovation

At Matini Building we can make vague concepts become exact reality, we provide full room renovations specializing in kitchens, bathrooms and laundries.


Waterproofing

We provide a complete service for all aspects of waterproofing protection to new and existing buildings with the application of membranes, liquids and sealants.


Floor Leveling

The most common problem we see with other tilers jobs are with the floor leveling, a lack of attention to small details here will mess up the whole process, but we take our time and do the job with no shortcuts taken, we use self-leveling concreate to perfectly level the floor


Carpentry

Our carpentry service provides the cutting, shaping and installation of building materials and timber frames.


Tiling Removal And Repair

If you want our services, chances are it's because you want a step up from your current tiling job, we can tear it out before we start our job if required.
